NCHS: Leading Causes of Death: United States

Ashley Toney | CS 498: Data Visualization - Summer 2019

Here, we'll aim to determine what the leading cause of death is in the United States and how it has changed over the past 18 years. This visualization aims to provide insight into the 10 leading causes of death in the United States as recorded over the past 18 years. The source of the data can be found at data.cdc.gov.


The Data

The dataset features death rate statistics captured from each of the 50 states between the years 1999-2016.

I. Dimensions
  • Year:
  • Cause Name: any of the 10 leading causes of death
  • State: data captured for the 50 states, Washington DC, and the United States
  • Age-adusted Death Reate: death rate adjusted to count for the aging population
